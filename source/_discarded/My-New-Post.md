I adopted [NodeJS][3] for my projects and on the way, I also discovered  [Angular][1] and [Sails JS][2] and finally for a Databases [DynamoDB][4] or [MongoDB][5] work perfectly for most projects. I am using a lot [DynamoDB][4] for convinience, as I have also adopted [Amazon Web Services][6] as the cloud platform for most of my projects.

For blogging [Hexo][7] is a pretty cool, which is what I am using on this blog. 

On the other hand I am also using a lot: [Apache 2][9], [PHP7][8], [MariaDB][11] and [CakePHP][10] as a framework.
